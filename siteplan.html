Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my portfolio and to provide information about me and my work. The website will include a home page that introduces myself and provides an overview of my skills and experience. There will also be a contact page where visitors can find my contact information and send me messages. Additionally, a projects page will display the various projects I have worked on, including descriptions and links to live demos or repositories.

Audience

The target audience for this website includes potential employers, clients, and collaborators who are interested in learning more about me and my work. The website will also be accessible to anyone who is interested in web development and design.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

A potential employer is looking to hire a web developer for a new project. They come across my website and are impressed by my portfolio and experience. They decide to reach out to me to discuss the opportunity further.

Scenario 2

A fellow web developer is interested in collaborating on a project. They visit my website to learn more about my skills and experience. They find my contact information and reach out to me to discuss potential collaboration.
